Introduction

Exhibition

The exhibition is Called AI + CARS where the dangers in self-driving or autonomous car is shown from aspects such as glitches, hackers/cyber threats, safety, lawsuit, job loss, environmental issues, etc.

Date & Time

The exhibition will take place on July 3rd to July 16th because 07/03 is when the first automobile was driven and 07/16 is the Artificial Intelligence Appreciation Day where discoveries in science, tech, healthcare, and AI is celebrated.

Location

Massachusetts Institute of Technology - Massachusetts is where the father of AI is born so looking in that state, MIT is one of the best schools in conducting research on AI, who also works with global organizations in the AI field, and has a motor program.

Motion

The animation starts out with a digital-like tracing effect to bring relation to technology, the motion then shows multiple glitch effect animation to present the disadvantages in AI embedded/Self-driving cars.
